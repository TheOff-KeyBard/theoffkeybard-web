---
title: The Ember Does Not Die
date: Mark 3, Turn of Embers, Cycle 6312
excerpt: Some fires do not begin when they are lit.
tag: Lore
---

There are those in Verasanth who do not learn fire.

They remember it.

The Ember-Touched carry something older than flame — not heat, not light, but persistence. The way fire clings to what it has touched. The way it refuses to end cleanly.

Their power does not arrive all at once.

It lingers.

A wound becomes warmth.  
Warmth becomes ember.  
Ember becomes something that does not go out.

The Archive teaches that this is a controllable state.

Most who carry it disagree.

Because once something begins to burn…

it does not always stop where you expect.
