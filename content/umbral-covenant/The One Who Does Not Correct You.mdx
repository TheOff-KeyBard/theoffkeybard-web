Serix Vaunt does not lie.

He simply does not correct you.

Those who speak with him often leave believing they understand what was said. This is permitted. Understanding, in Verasanth, is rarely as stable as it first appears.

He does not explain the city.

He reveals where explanation fails.

A question is answered with something adjacent to truth. A certainty is met with a detail that does not fit. A belief is returned, altered just enough that it cannot remain intact.

He watches what follows.

To Serix, corruption is not a force to be resisted.

It is a lens.

What it reveals is not new. It is what was already there, stripped of whatever comfort once made it bearable.

Most turn away from this.

Some do not.

Those who remain interest him.

He does not guide them.

He allows them to continue.

And when they begin to see what he sees…

he does not confirm it.

He simply does not deny it.
