There are those in Verasanth who move unseen.

The Shadowbound are not among them.

They are seen.

Just not where they should be.

The darkness they carry is not concealment. It does not hide them. It bends around them, distorts the moment, shifts perception just enough that what is present is not understood in time.

A figure in the corner of the eye.

A movement that does not complete.

A presence that was not there… until it already was.

They do not enter shadow.

They move with it.

The strike comes from the space between noticing and knowing. From the instant the world hesitates, unsure of what it is seeing, and chooses too late.

Those who survive speak of speed.

Others speak of silence.

Neither is correct.

It is something closer to absence.

A gap where something should have been—and was not.

Until it was.
