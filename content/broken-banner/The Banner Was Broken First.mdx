title: The Banner Was Broken First
date: Mark 15, Turn of Stone, Cycle 6312
excerpt: Discipline is what survives when strength fails.
tag: Lore
---

The Broken Banner is not a symbol of defeat.

It is a record.

Once, it flew whole. That much is agreed upon. What broke it — when, and why — is less certain. The guild preserves it not as a relic of loss, but as a reminder that something must fail before it can be understood.

Within the Banner’s walls, battle is not chaos.

It is repetition.

Measured strikes. Controlled breathing. The same movement performed until it cannot be performed incorrectly.

The city does not reward strength.

It tests it.

The Banner exists to ensure that when that test comes…

something remains standing.
