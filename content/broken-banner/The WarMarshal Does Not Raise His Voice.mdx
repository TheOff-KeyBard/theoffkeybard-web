---
title: The WarMarshal Does Not Raise His Voice
date: Mark 9, Turn of Stone, Cycle 6312
excerpt: Control is louder than rage.
tag: Lore
---

WarMarshal Garruk Stonehide does not shout.

He does not need to.

He stands like something that has already survived what the rest of the room fears. His armor is scarred, but never neglected. His movements are deliberate, measured, as if every action has already been tested against failure.

Recruits expect brutality.

They find restraint.

Garruk does not teach how to fight.

He teaches when not to.

Those who mistake fury for strength do not last long under his command.

The city breaks many things.

He is interested in what remains after.
