title: The Body Remembers the Blow
date: Mark 2, Turn of Stone, Cycle 6312
excerpt: Some people do not learn to fight. They remember surviving.
tag: Lore
---

There are those in Verasanth who do not train for battle.

They arrive already shaped by it.

The Ironblood carry the memory of impact — the shock of force, the ringing aftermath, the moment between falling and choosing not to. Their strength is not speed, nor fury.

It is continuation.

Where others hesitate, they advance. Where others break, they endure. Not because they are stronger — but because something in them refuses the idea of stopping.

The Banner teaches discipline.

The body already knows the rest.
