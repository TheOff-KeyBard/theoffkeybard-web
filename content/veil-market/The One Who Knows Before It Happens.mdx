Lirael Quickstep rarely asks questions.

By the time others think to ask them, she already has the answers.

Not all of them. Not perfectly. But enough.

Enough to know when something is about to change. Enough to understand which direction that change will take, and who will be caught unprepared when it does.

She does not chase information.

She arranges it.

A word overheard becomes a pattern. A pattern becomes a prediction. A prediction becomes a decision made before anyone realizes there was a choice to make.

Those who deal with her often leave believing they were in control.

This is permitted.

Lirael does not need to be seen guiding events.

Only to ensure they move where she intends.

The city runs on desperation, on fear, on the quiet exchange of things people do not want known.

She listens to all of it.

Not because she trusts it.

But because somewhere in the noise…

something useful always emerges.
