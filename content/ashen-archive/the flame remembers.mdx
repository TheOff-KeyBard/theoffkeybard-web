There are those in Verasanth who do not learn fire.

They remember it.

The Ember-Touched carry something older than flame — not heat, not light, but memory. The way fire lingers in stone long after it cools. The way ash still whispers what it once consumed.

They are warm, at first.

Then not.

The Archive claims it teaches containment. That flame can be shaped, studied, understood.

But every Ember-Touched knows the truth.

Fire is never contained.

It is only convinced.
