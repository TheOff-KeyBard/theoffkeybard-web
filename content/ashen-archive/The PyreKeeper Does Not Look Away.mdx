High PyreKeeper Vaelith Xyrelle does not fear the city.

That alone makes people uneasy.

She moves through Verasanth as if it were something to be understood rather than survived — through drowned tunnels, unstable caverns, places where the stone hums with something older than the streets above.

Some relics respond to her.

They glow. They tremble. Sometimes they whisper.

She never explains why.

The Archive trusts her.

The city tolerates her.

Neither relationship appears stable.
