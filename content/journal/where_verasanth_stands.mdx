## 🔥 Where Verasanth Stands

Verasanth is open.

Not finished. Not settled. Not safe.

But open.

You can enter the city. You can walk its early paths. You can speak to the people who keep it running — or failing to. You can descend beneath the surface and begin to see what kind of place this really is.

That matters, because Verasanth is no longer just an idea I’m talking about.

It is live.  
It is playable.  
And it has started to remember.

---

### 🧭 What Exists Right Now

The foundation is real.

You can move through the city and into its early descent. The tavern, the streets, the first pull downward — they are there. The world is no longer just written around the game. It is inside it.

The city’s key voices are there too.

Phase A NPC dialogue is fully authored and verified in the live Worker. That means the core figures you meet early on are no longer leaning on inference or filler. They speak from structured memory now, not improvisation. The Claude bleed-through that used to muddy some of those interactions has been closed. For those characters, what they say is now intentional.

Beyond that, the guild leaders have been authored as well. The next layer of Verasanth’s truth is not waiting to be imagined — it is waiting to be surfaced.

---

### ⚔️ What You Can Do In Game

- **Explore the city**  
  Move through Verasanth and begin to understand its shape.

- **Speak to authored NPCs**  
  Key figures now operate on structured dialogue systems with memory, trust, and progression-aware responses.

- **Align with a guild**  
  Each guild represents a function of the city — not just a faction.

- **Descend into early layers**  
  The opening stretch of the descent exists and is playable.

- **Engage with early combat systems**  
  Weapons, armor, and instincts are present — and beginning to matter.

---

### ⚙️ What Is In Progress

This is the current focus:

- **Economy (NPC-driven)**  
  Vendors are being built out with baseline gear and meaningful purchase paths. Player-driven economy comes later.

- **Combat + Equipment Integration**  
  Stats, weapons, and armor are being wired into real combat impact.

- **Instinct + Build Identity**  
  Instincts are being pushed toward distinct playstyles rather than passive flavor.

- **Guild Depth (Phase B)**  
  Guild leaders are authored and ready to deepen the system.

- **System Stability**  
  The goal right now is not expansion — it is making what exists feel solid.

---

### 🧠 What This Is

This isn’t a finished game.

It’s a **live world under construction** — one you can already step into.

Some things will feel incomplete.  
Some things will feel intentional.  

Part of the experience is figuring out which is which.
